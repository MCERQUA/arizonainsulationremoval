- **Impact:** Sophisticated buyers question our carrier quality
- **Action Needed:** Add carrier ratings/badges to homepage and key pages

### 3. Specialized Endorsements Missing
RPS explicitly lists endorsements we don't mention:
- Pollution Liability
- Employee Benefits Liability  
- Blanket Additional Insured
- Primary & Non-Contributory
- Waiver of Subrogation
- Care, Custody & Control ("on the hook" coverage)

**Impact:** We appear to offer only basic coverage vs. their comprehensive options

### 4. Industry-Specific Language
- **RPS Uses:** Technical terms like "on the hook," "upset/overturn," "boom damage"
- **We Use:** More generic insurance terminology
- **Impact:** Less credibility with crane operators who know these terms
